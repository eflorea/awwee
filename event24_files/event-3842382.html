It's easy to create a LinkedIn profile and scroll through the profiles of the people in your network, but when it comes to posting status updates, accepting connections from strangers, job searching, participating in groups, and giving/receiving endorsements and recommendations, it can get overwhelming pretty quickly.

Join us for this 1-hour workshop with Cecily Hastings, LinkedIn Relationship Manager for the State of California, as she shares best practices for your profile, professional networking, and building your personal brand using LinkedIn.
