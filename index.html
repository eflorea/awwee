MEMBER BENEFITS

MEMBER-ONLY EVENTS & PROGRAMS

Members have access to attend special member-only events and to participate in member-only programs such as The Exchange, AWWEE's annual mentoring program.

FREE OR DISCOUNTED EVENT REGISTRATION

We host over 40 events across California and via webinar each year. Most events are free for members to attend and typically include refreshments. Registration for more costly events is discounted for members.

A NETWORK OF 2000+ MEMBERS

AWWEE is constantly growing and offering its members new services, educational opportunities and an expanding network of connections.

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

Most AWWEE events are organized by members who volunteer to serve on a regional leadership team. Teams also occasionally organize volunteer events to give back to the communities where we live and work.
