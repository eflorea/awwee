Regional Leadership Teams

The majority of AWWEE’s educational events are organized by teams of members who volunteer to participate on one of eight regional teams across California.  Each team organizes 5+ events within their region each year.  Team members typically meet monthly by phone to brainstorm event ideas and delegate event planning responsibilities.  Team leaders organize team meetings, ensure team events meet association guidelines, and contribute their perspective to overall association management and strategic planning.
