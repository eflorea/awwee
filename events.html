Important message from AWWEE Board President, Gwenneth O’Hara regarding upcoming events amid concerns of COVID-19:

April 13, 2020 update

Dear AWWEE Community –

I hope this message finds you healthy, safe and settling in to new routines.  While we can hardly wait to see you all in-person again soon, we would never jeopardize your safety.  All in-person AWWEE events continue to be postponed until further notice.  The AWWEE Board of Directors continues to monitor the COVID-19 situation closely and will decide when to begin scheduling in-person events again when we are certain it will be safe to do so.  Even then, we recognize that it may take some time before we are comfortable gathering in close proximity, and we will be mindful of that when we plan.  In the meantime, we have organized lots of opportunities for the AWWEE community to stay connected during this time.  We may be physically distant, but we are in this together.

We intend to reschedule postponed events when we can safely.  If you registered for an event that is now postponed, we will save your spot for the rescheduled date.  If you are not able to attend at that time, we kindly ask that you consider donating the registration fee you paid to support AWWEE’s continued operations, but we certainly understand if you prefer a refund and will gladly accommodate. 

Take care of yourselves.  We look forward to seeing you again in-person soon!
Gwenn

Cancellations

Registration fees will be refunded for cancellations received 3 days prior to the event. For late cancellations and no shows, registration fees paid will be available as a credit for future event registration or applied to membership dues within the calendar year. Any credit not used at the end of the year will be treated as a donation to the Association of Women in Water, Energy and Environment.

To cancel your registration for an event, email info@awwee.test

AWWEE members receive 20% off registration for the 8th World Sustainability Forum.  Choose the Academics registration option.  Proof of membership is required.  Visit the Member Access page to log in and download your AWWEE member ID card.
