JOIN THE ASSOCIATION OF WOMEN IN WATER, ENERGY AND ENVIRONMENT

The Association of Women in Water, Energy and Environment (AWWEE) was created to promote the professional growth and success of women working in the water, energy and environment fields.

As a member, you will receive free or discounted registration for AWWEE educational and networking events held across California throughout the year. 

You will also have access to member-only events, our annual mentoring program, as well as opportunities to help plan future events if you choose to volunteer on one of our regional teams.

Employees of Existing Organizational Members

If your organization has a Platinum or Sustaining level organizational membership, please do not submit a membership application.  Instead, please subscribe to our mailing list using your company email address to be added to your organization’s membership.
